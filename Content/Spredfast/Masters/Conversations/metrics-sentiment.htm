Sentiment

Sentiment data is provided through Social Mention. The Sentiment view shows sentiment trends on selected keywords over the selected date range.To collect sentiment data, you must create a search stream containing keywords using Social Mention as the channel.

Sentiment data obtained through Social Mention is obtained each time the sentiment stream is updated, but is summarized and updated in the once a day.

Sentiment data is different from other data because sentiment scores have an impact that extends beyond the date the item was posted. For example, a positive item that is viewed days after its posting date can still generate a positive reaction. Therefore, sentiment scores are calculated using the posted date and a subsequent historical timeframe, depending on the type of content. Social Mention uses the following:

  • Lifetime of one month: blog posts, news items, videos, audio files, pictures and questions.
  • Lifetime of one week: microblog posts, bookmarks, comments, and events.

For each keyword, the system displays the following:

Sentiment (ratio)

Ratio of positive items to negative items.

Positive Items

Net new number of content items containing the keyword that were deemed positive by Social Mention.

Click on any highlighted number to see specific stream items that resulted in the “positive” determination.

Positive Score

Sum of the sentiment scores for all of the individual positive items.

Neutral Items

Net new number of content items containing the keyword that were deemed neutral by Social Mention.

Click on any highlighted number to see specific stream items that resulted in the “neutral” determination.

Negative Items

Net new number of content items containing the keyword that were deemed as negative by Social Mention.

Click on any highlighted number to see specific stream items that resulted in the “negative” determination.

Negative Score

Sum of the sentiment scores for all of the individual negative items.

Activity

In the Sentiment view, the Activity line shows all activity published for the Initiative over the date range (the same Roll Up value contained in Detailed Analytics) It is NOT activity published containing the specific term/keyword.

Engagement

In the Sentiment view, the Engagement line shows all engagement received by the Initiative over the date range (the same Roll Up value contained in Detailed Analytics). It is NOT engagement received by items containing the keyword.
