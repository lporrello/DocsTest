Reply/Reply All

Now or at a scheduled time. To reply to all the people mentioned in a tweet, click Reply, then, in the Text field, click the new Reply All tab. The tab shows all the people to whom the reply will be sent.

Retweet

Now or at a scheduled time. Two types of retweet are supported in : “Native” and Editable. “Native” Retweet is similar to clicking Retweet on Twitter—the message is posted exactly as written (it cannot be edited) and no engagement metrics are collected for it. Editable retweets consist of RT@handle, plus the message text. These can be edited prior to publishing, and collects engagement metrics on them.

Who Determines Permitted Tweet Types?
Company Admins determine which type(s) of retweets are available to their users. See Twitter Admin Settings.

Direct Message/Reply to Direct Message

The user must be following you in order for you to be able to direct message.

Mark a Tweet as a “Favorite”

Click Favorite, select the accounts to use to Favorite the item (for all accounts, click Available Twitter accounts), then click Favorite. Once an item has been marked as a Favorite via , it can be Unfavorited as well. The history of “Favorite” actions taken in is shown in the stream item entry (account, date, and time). Note that the Favorites count shown is not the total number of times the item was Favorited on Twitter; it is the number of times the item was Favorited from .
