Bazaarvoice Streams

  1. From the Stream Library menu, select Create Streams.
  2. Select Bazaarvoice from the drop-down menu.
  3. Use the Search box to find products and categories to appear in the stream. You can search by Category Name, Category ID, Product Name, and Product ID. Exact text match is required; partial matching is not supported. Click on search results to add them to the stream.
  4. When the stream is first created in the Inbox, the 100 most recent reviews are obtained.
  5. Select Ratings categories; click All Ratings to include all ratings values, or click Individual Ratings, then select one or more specific ratings (one to five stars).
  6. Select Review Feedback categories.
    Review feedback refers to readers’ impressions of whether the review was helpful or not. If 50% or more review readers gave the review positive feedback (thumbs up), the review is considered “Helpful.” If 49% or more readers gave negative feedback (thumbs down), the review is considered “Unhelpful.” “No feedback available” means that no readers have provided positive or negative feedback about the review.
  7. Click Add.
  8. Click Done.
