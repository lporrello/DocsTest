Scheduled Reports

All company-wide analytics can generate scheduled reports to any email address, regardless of whether they are a user.

Schedule a Report

  1. From any Company-wide report, set any filters for your report.
  2. In the top right corner, click the Clock icon.
  3. Expand the Dashboard Tabs drop-down and select all reports that should be included.
  4. Expand the Reports drop-down and click Daily to receive a report each day.
  5. Expand the Reports drop-down and click Weekly to receive a report on a weekly basis. Use the Every drop-down to dictate the frequency and the On Day drop-down to select a day of the week.
  6. Expand the Reports drop-down and click Monthly to receive a report each day. Use the On drop-down to indicate whether to send based on the Date of the Month (e.g., the 1st of every month) or Day of the Month (e.g., Mondays). Use the subsequently named drop-down to select a specific day or date.
  7. In the Time drop-down menu, set the time the report should send. Time zones are based on your settings.
  8. Click Add recipients or define custom subject and message to make further specifications.
  9. Enter additional recipients in the Send a Copy To field.
  10. In the Email Subject field, enter a subject for the email.
  11. In the Email Message field, enter a message to accompany each report.
  12. Click Schedule.
