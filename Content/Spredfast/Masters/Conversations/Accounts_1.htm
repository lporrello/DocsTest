Accounts

Followers

The total number of Followers for all Instagram accounts in the Initiatives. The number is the most recent total; click on the number to see Followers per account. The graph shows the trend in Follower count over multiple weeks; click on a week’s data point to see the count for each account during a specific week. This data is provided by Instagram.

Following

The total number of Instagram Users being followed by all Instagram accounts in the Initiatives. The number is the most recent total; click on the number to see the number of Users being Followed, per account. The graph shows the trend in “Following” count over multiple weeks; click on a week’s data point to see the count for each account during a specific week. This data is provided by Instagram.

Total Media

The count of media (photos and videos) for all Instagram accounts in the Initiatives as of the last day of the date range. The number is most recent total for all accounts on the last day in the date range; click on it to see the number per account. The graph shows the trend in publishing activity over multiple weeks; click on a week’s data point to see media published per account for that specific week. This data is provided by Instagram.
