Total Reach

Counts the number of people your Initiative has affected. Current Network includes those who have “officially” joined your network by becoming a friend, fan, follower, and the like. Potential Impressions includes those who might have come into contact with your content through an act of sharing by a third party, such as a mention or a retweet. Combining Current Network and Potential Impressions provides a sense of the total number of people you reached, or could have reached, on a given day. Though there can be overlap between the categories, such is the nature of impression data.

Measures interaction of your audience with your published content, including actions such as clicking through a link, leaving a comment, liking the content, replying, retweeting, and adding a rating. We can capture full engagement metrics on actions published through the system. For auto-imported content, most interaction is captured, but some ability to track link click-throughs is limited.
