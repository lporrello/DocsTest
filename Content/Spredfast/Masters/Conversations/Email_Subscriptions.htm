Email Subscriptions

Select the circumstances under which you want to receive notification emails from :

  • Daily digest of upcoming activity on your Initiatives
  • Alerts for activity that need action within the next hour
  • Notification when another user assigns an event to you
  • Notification when there is new outgoing content waiting for your approval.
  • Notification when there are new highlighted items waiting for your review
  • Notification when there are new items in your Primary Collections. You can set the frequency of notifications in the Send Inbox Notifications Every menu.
