A bunch of messages to post at the same time on different days the same (all Facebook, for example) different each time Series, which creates the message shells and puts them on the agenda. Click the circled ... (ellipsis) icon next to the channel icons, and choose Series
A bunch of messages to post on different days the same (all Twitter, for example) the same every time Repost. Messages containing the same content aren’t allowed to repost within 24 hours (plus 5 minutes) In the Schedule pane, click Repost).
A bunch of messages to post on the same day or different days different (both Facebook and Twitter, for example) the same, or mostly the same Copy message after I’ve created the first one, or change channels “on the fly” as I’m creating the first one. From the confirmation screen, or from the Agenda, click Copy Message.
A bunch of automated retweets to post at designated intervals after an original has posted the same (all Twitter) the same, or mostly the same, in the format of “RT @ [credit] +text” Retweet, while creating the original in the Twitter message creation screen.
