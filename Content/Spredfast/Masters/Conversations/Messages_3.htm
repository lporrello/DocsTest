LinkedIn Average Engagement

The number is the average engagement: the sum of all engagement (Bitly clicks, comments, Likes, shares for Companies, and Bitly clicks, comments and Likes for Profiles) received by all messages published during the date range by all LinkedIn accounts (Profile and Company Pages) in the Initiatives, divided by the number of messages published. Click on the number to see average engagement received by messages published by each account. The graph shows the trend in engagement activity over multiple weeks; click on a week’s data point to see average engagement received by each account during that week. This data is calculated by .

Click on the number to see impressions received by messages published by each account. LinkedIn Engagement Rate

Engagement rate (percentage) is the number of people who clicked on, liked, commented on, or shared a message, divided by the number of unique people who saw it (impressions). Click on the percentage to see the numbers used to calculate it, for each account. The graph shows the trend over multiple weeks; click on a data point to see counts for each account during that week. This data is calculated by Spredfast.

LinkedIn Engagement by Type

The Engagement number is the sum of all engagement (Bitly clicks, comments, likes, shares) received by all messages published during the date range by all LinkedIn accounts (Profile and Company Pages) in the Initiatives. Click on the number to see engagement sums per account. For each engagement type (Bitly clicks, Comments, Likes, Shares), the top-level numbers represent the sum for all accounts; click on the number for each type to see engagement values of that type for each account. Note that Share data is only available for Company accounts.

  • Bitly Clicks—Number of clicks made on (Bitly) links embedded in messages published via or auto-imported, for all LinkedIn accounts that are part of the Initiatives. This data is provided by Bitly.
  • Comments—Number of comments received by all messages published via or auto-imported, by all LinkedIn accounts that are part of the Initiatives. This data is provided by LinkedIn.
  • Likes—Number of likes received by all messages published via or auto-imported, for all LinkedIn accounts that are part of the Initiatives. This data is provided by LinkedIn.
  • Shares—Number of shares received by all messages published via or auto-imported, for all LinkedIn Company accounts that are part of the Initiatives. This data is provided by LinkedIn. Note that Share data is not available for Profile or Group accounts.

Bitly Link Clicks vs. LinkedIn Link Clicks
Click counts on LinkedIn messages are provided to by two sources: Bitly and LinkedIn. The Bitly value is used for engagement calculations and summaries. LinkedIn click counts are NOT included in engagement calculations in Spredfast Conversations, because many of these links are also Bitly links, which could result in counting a link click twice.

Includes the number of messages that used the labels and the amount of engagement (average and count) received by the messages. A blank in the Label field indicates that no label was used. A message may appear in multiple counts if it has multiple labels applied to it.
