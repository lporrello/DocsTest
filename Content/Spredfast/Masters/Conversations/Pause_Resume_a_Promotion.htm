Pause or Resume a Facebook Promotion

Users who have the ability to promote Facebook messages can click in the message’s Promotion status to pause and resume an active promotion.

  1. On the Calendar > Agenda tab, from within a calendar entry for a promoted message, click the Promotion status entry for an active promotion.
  2. In the Promotion tab, Change Status field, click the Paused radio button.
  3. Click Save. The promotion status changes to paused. You can reactivate it by clicking the Pending status box, and then licking the Active radio button.
