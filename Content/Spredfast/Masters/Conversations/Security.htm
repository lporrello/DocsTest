Password and Account Security

These general rules apply to how passwords and email addresses are changed and managed in .

  • Users cannot change their passwords without entering their current password
  • When a user (with appropriate permissions) changes another person’s password, she must enter her own password first
  • Only Company Owners can change other users’ email addresses. Company Admins cannot.
  • Admins cannot change a person’s password while in impersonation mode. (Admin Panel > Users > Impersonate). Instead, Admins can edit a user’s password through the Admin Panel by finding the user within his company, and clicking the Edit icon.

These requirements are implemented differently for companies that require Single Sign On (the Make Single Sign On mandatory checkbox on the My Company > Security tab is checked) and those that do not.

Companies that Require Single Sign On

Company Owners

  • Can change their own passwords on the My Account page.
  • Can change their own passwords on the My Company > Users tab.
  • Cannot change other users’ passwords using .
  • Can change their own email address on the My Account page and the My Company > Users tab.
  • Can change email addresses of other users on the My Company > Users tab.

Company Admins

  • Cannot change their own passwords on the My Account page or the My Company > Users tab.
  • Cannot change other users’ passwords on the My Company > Users tab.
  • Cannot change their email address on the My Account page or the My Company > Users tab.
  • Cannot change other users’ email addresses on the My Company > Users tab.

Users

  • Cannot change their own password, or the passwords of others, from any location. Users must contact their administrator or the third-party password manager.
  • Cannot change their email address, or the email addresses of other users, from any location.

Companies with Optional Single Sign On, or No Single Sign On

Company Owners

  • Can change their own passwords on the My Account page.
  • Can change other users’ passwords on the My Company > Users tab.
  • Can change their own passwords on the My Company > Users tab.
  • Can change their own email address on the My Account page and the My Company > Users tab.
  • Can change email addresses of other users on the My Company > Users tab.

Company Admins

  • Can change their own passwords on the My Account page or the My Company > Users tab.
  • Can change other users’ passwords on the My Company > Users tab.
  • Cannot change their email address on the My Account page or the My Company > Users tab.
  • Cannot change other users’ email addresses on the My Company > Users tab.

Users

  • Can change their own passwords on the My Account page, but cannot change passwords for any other users.
  • Cannot change their email addresses, or the email addresses of other users, from any location.

Admins

  • Can change their own passwords from any location.
  • Can change other users’ passwords from the My Company > Users tab or the Admin Panel (Admin Panel > Companies > [company name] > Users > Edit).
  • Can change their own email address from any location.
  • Can change others’ email addresses from the My Company > Users tab or the Admin Panel.
