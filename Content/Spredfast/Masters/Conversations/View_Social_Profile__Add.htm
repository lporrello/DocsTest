View Social Profile and Add to Influencers

If an item has an identifiable author, click on the avatar to see the author's Social Profile. The profile will contain the following:

  • Any available biographical data included on their social network profile (such as a Klout score)
  • The history of interaction with the user
  • notes that have been added to the author.

You can choose to add the author to the Initiative's list of Influencers, “follow” the author (for Twitter), or go to the author’s profile on the social channel. displays the avatar of the account used to interact with the profile.

When an author is identified as an influencer, a gold circle appears around the author’s avatar. When notes about the author are added, an icon appears beneath the avatar.
