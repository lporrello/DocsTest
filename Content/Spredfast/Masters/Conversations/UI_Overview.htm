Main Navigation Page Menus and Icons
Icon Description
Product Options—If other products have been enabled for your organization, you can log in to them directly from .

Inbox—Click to display the Inbox and moderate your social properties
Calendar—Click to see published messages and messages scheduled for publication
Page assets—Click to see your Company’s Content Center, where text and image assets are stored.
Bar graph—Click to see analytics on published messages.
Hammer and brush—Click to create custom applications, such as quizzes and polls.
Question mark—Click to access the User Guide, What’s New, and the Social Service Station
Plus sign—Click to provide feedback, or contact Support
Left-facing chevrons—Click to collapse the main navigation panel
Right-facing chevrons—Click to expand the main navigation panel
