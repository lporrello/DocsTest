Instagram Performance Dashboard

Audience Size

The sum of audience members (Followers) for all Instagram accounts in the Initiatives. The percentage change (up or down) is calculated by comparing the data from the current date range to the data from the prior (equivalent) date range. For example, if the date range is 60 days, the percentage is calculated by comparing the current data to data from the previous 60 day period. Click the percentage to see values for each account in the current period, previous period, change (count) and change (percentage). The graph shows the trend in Follower count over multiple weeks; click on a week’s data point to see the Follower counts for each account for that specific week.These values are calculated by .

Published Media

The sum of all auto-imported media (photos, videos and comments) for all accounts in the Initiatives over the date range. The percentage change (up or down) is calculated by comparing the data from the current date range to the data from the prior (equivalent) date range. For example, if the date range is 60 days, the percentage is calculated by comparing the current data to data from the previous 60 day period. Click the percentage to see each channel’s values for the current period, previous period, change (count) and change (percentage). These values are calculated by . The graph shows the trend in published media over multiple weeks; click on a week’s data point to see the published media counts for each account for that specific week.

Engagement on Media

The total (count) of all engagement (likes and comments) received by media that was auto-imported during the date range, for all accounts in the Initiatives. The percentage change (up or down) is calculated by comparing the data from the current date range to the data from the prior (equivalent) date range. For example, if the date range is 60 days, the percentage is calculated by comparing the current data to data from the previous 60 day period. Click the percentage to see values for each account in the current period, previous period, change (count) and change (percentage). The graph shows the trend in engagement over multiple weeks; click on a week’s data point to see the engagement counts for each account for that specific week. These values are calculated by .

Highest Performing Content

Top 3 media (photos or videos) published during the date range that received the highest engagement (cumulative since publication).

Lowest Performing Content

Bottom 3 media (photos or videos) published during date range that received the lowest engagement (cumulative since publication).

Published Media

The sum of items (photos videos, and comments) auto-imported from all Instagram accounts in the Initiatives, over the date range. The number is the total for all accounts over the date range; click on it to see the number per account. This data is calculated by . Note that comments are included as published items, but do not collect engagement metrics.

Engagement

The total of all engagement (likes and comments) received by all auto-imported media published during the date range by all Instagram accounts in the Initiatives. Click on the number to see engagement totals per account.

Comments

Number of comments received by all auto-imported media, for all Instagram accounts that are part of the Initiatives. This data is provided by Instagram.

Likes

Number of likes received by all auto-imported media, for all Instagram accounts that are part of the Initiatives. This data is provided by Instagram.

Top Media by Engagement

The top 50 individual media (photos and videos) based on the amount of engagement (count) they received, across all accounts in the Initiatives. (Note that when there are duplicates of a message in the list, it often means the item was published through multiple Initiatives.)
