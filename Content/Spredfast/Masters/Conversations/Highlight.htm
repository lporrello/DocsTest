Highlight

Highlighting a stream item for others in the Initiative lets them know that you think the item is of interest to them. The people for whom you highlight an item receive an email and reminders about the item, and the item appears in their (and your) Highlighted stream (see Highlighted Stream). A count of items that have been highlighted specifically for you appears in your To Do list.

The Highlight feature can help teams of users manage conversations. Items can be highlighted for individual users, or teams of users. When the item appears in the user’s Highlighted stream, the user can “accept” the item and take action on it. Once action has been completed, the user can “Archive” the item.

For information on creating Highlight teams, see Highlight Teams.

Highlight a Stream Item

  1. From within an item in a Inbox stream, click the More actions menu and select Highlight item.
  2. In the Highlight Item dialog, click on a Highlight team, or individual users (or, drag and drop them into the right-hand panel) to add them to the “Highlight for” list. Use the Search box to locate users; remember that the user’s Workspace must include access to the Inbox for the user to be eligible to receive highlighted items.
  3. You can add labels or notes to the item; click Additional Options and click Labels or Notes.
  4. Click Save.
