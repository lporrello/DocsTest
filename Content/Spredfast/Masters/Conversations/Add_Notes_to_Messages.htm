Add Notes to Multiple Messages

  1. From the Calendar > Agenda tab, hover over the upper left-hand corner of an item until a checkbox appears. Click the checkbox to select the item. To select all the day’s messages, click the checkbox to the left of the Details menu. The Add Note button appears in the Agenda header.
  2. Click Add Note.
  3. In the Add Note, Add a Note field, type text for the note.
  4. Click Save.
