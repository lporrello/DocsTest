Standard Streams

Standard streams in the system are as follows:

  • Primary Collection—a collection of multiple streams determined to be the most important for the Initiative. Note that stream items are displayed/retained in the Inbox for 90 days. After 90 days, an item can be found by looking at its source stream separately. Use the drop-down menu next to the Inbox heading to filter the contents displayed in this stream for Archived (for the whole Initiative), Read, or Unread items.
  • Highlighted—a collection of items that have been highlighted for you, or that you have highlighted for someone else. Use the drop-down menu next to the Highlighted label to show items you have archived for yourself.
  • All Activity—a collection of all actions that have been published from the Initiative, from newest to oldest.
  • Recent Engagement—a collection of items published from the Initiative that have received the most recent actionable engagement.
  • Hidden/Deleted—a collection of items that have been deleted from their native platform using , items marked as deleted within , items identifies as deleted, and Facebook items that have been hidden on Facebook using .

In addition to these standard streams, you can create custom groups of streams, called Collections.
