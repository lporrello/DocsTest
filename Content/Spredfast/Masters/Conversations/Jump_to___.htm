Jump to...

Use the Jump to menu to go directly to...

 

  • a specific date and time within the stream. If multiple items meet the date/time criteria, jumps to the oldest item. If no items match exactly, jumps to the closest one.
  • the last new item above the New item separator (which appears between groups of items)
  • the item at the top of the stream.
  • a shared bookmark, if one has been added to the stream or shared collection. See Add, Move, Remove Shared Bookmark.

In the Highlighted stream, because items do not arrive chronologically, you can Jump to the date and time the item was most recently highlighted.

Go to a Specific Date and Time Within a Inbox Stream

  1. From the stream header, click Jump to > Date/time. A date/time selector appears beneath the header.
  1. Use the calendar to select the date (for a maximum of 30 days back) and the clock to select a time. You can also use terms such as “Next Thursday” or “Wednesday the 3rd.”
  2. Click Go.

To return to the top of the stream, click the Jump to icon, and select Top of stream.

Select All
If you use the Select All checkbox (to choose multiple items to process at once) after using the Jump to menu, all items viewed since jumping are selected.
