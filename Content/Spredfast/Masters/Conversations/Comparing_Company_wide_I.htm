Comparing Company-wide / Initiative-level Analytics

Within , performance metrics can be evaluated at the Initiative level and company-wide. The company-wide views, provided in the dashboards, let executives compare social properties, users, and engagement at a high level. Initiative-level views, shown in the and the can be useful to those managing specific projects or campaigns.
