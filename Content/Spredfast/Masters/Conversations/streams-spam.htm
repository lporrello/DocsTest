Hidden/Deleted Stream

Items that have been hidden from Facebook via , deleted from their native platform via , identified by as having been deleted, or marked as deleted within are shown in the Hidden/Deleted stream.

The following items can be deleted from their native platform via :

  • Facebook wall posts (and comments on those posts)
  • Google+ posts, and comments on those posts
  • Your own tweets from Twitter
  • Comments on Wordpress posts
  • Comments in LinkedIn Groups discussion topics and LinkedIn Company pages.
  • Weibo posts, comments, replies and reposts

See Respond to Stream Items for more information on deleting items from each channel.

See the User Guide for more information on deleting items from each channel.

When a user summons the All Comments dialog, compares the current content with the former content; if items are missing, it adds them to the Hidden/Deleted stream.

In situations when the items cannot be deleted from their native platform using , you have the option to mark them “deleted” using the Delete in Spredfast button, so that displays them in the Hidden/Deleted stream for tracking purposes.

Hidden/Deleted Stream Replaces Former Spam Stream
Note that at the 4.05 release, the designation “Spam” was deprecated, and the Spam stream renamed to the Hidden/Deleted stream. Items that were marked as spam prior to 4.05 do not appear in the new Hidden/Deleted stream, unless they were also marked as Deleted.
