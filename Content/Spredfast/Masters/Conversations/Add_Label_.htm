Add Label

Add a label to an item. Click in the field, and begin to type a label. If the label is already present in , it appears in the list; select it to add it to the message. Labels that have been designated as “top labels” appear in bold within the list. Some users are able to create new labels from this screen. Type the new label in the field and press Enter; the new label appears is available for others in the company to use.

If Crimson Hexagon is enabled for your organization, when you add a label to an item in the Inbox, you can also assign a Crimson Hexagon category. the Crimson Hexagon category is also treated as a label in .
