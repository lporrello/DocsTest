Amplify

Amplify stream item content by publishing it to Facebook, Google+, Twitter, or LinkedIn. A user must have ability to publish microblogs on the channel to use the Amplify feature.

Amplify a Inbox Item on Another Social Channel

  1. From within a stream item, click the More actions > Amplify menu.
  2. Select Amplify on Facebook, Amplify on Twitter, Amplify on LinkedIn, or Amplify on Google+. A channel-appropriate dialog appears containing content from the item.
  3. In the text box, add text and/or edit the content as appropriate for the channel (such as shortening it for Twitter).
  4. In the Accounts pane, choose the channel accounts through which to amplify the review.
  5. Click Additional Options to add processing notes and labels. If you’re using Amplify on Facebook, and have the ability to promote, you can also promote the message in the Newsfeed.
  6. Choose Scheduling options, and then click Save Draft or Publish ASAP.
