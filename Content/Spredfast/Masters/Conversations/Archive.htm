Archive

Within , you can “archive” items from the Primary Collection and from the Highlighted stream. The archive function on the Primary Collection stream is Initiative-wide, meaning that if one user in the Initiative archives an item from her Primary Collection, the item appears in the other users’ Primary Collection archives, too.

The archive function on the Highlighted stream is a personal user archive; items placed in the archive by one user do not automatically appear in another user’s Highlight archive.

Archived items are a subset of “read” items; if an item is archived is automatically marked as read.

  • Use Restore from Archive to return items to the Primary Collection or Highlighted Stream.
