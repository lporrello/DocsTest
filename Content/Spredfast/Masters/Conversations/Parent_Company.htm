Parent Company

A “Parent Company” is a structural concept within used to group multiple organizations (companies) under a single entity (parent company). Each company can have one parent. Each parent can have multiple children. Once a parent company has been configured in the system, you can associate its child companies with it. You cannot change a current company into a parent company, and a parent company cannot have its own Initiatives or users; it is a “container” for companies only.

Create a Parent Company

Company Configuration
Associating a new company with a parent can also be done when the (child) company is first added to .

  1. From the Admin Panel, Company menu, click Parent Companies.
  2. Click Add New Parent Company.
  3. In the Name field, enter the company name.
  4. (Optional) In the Salesforce ID field, provide the ID used for the Parent company in Salesforce.
  5. Click Save. The Parent company appears in the list.

Associate a Company with a Parent Company

  1. From the Admin Panel, Company menu, click Companies.
  2. In the list of Companies, find the one(s) that you want to associate with the parent. Click the checkbox to the left of the company name. (To select all, click the checkbox to the left of the Actions menu.)
  3. Click the arrow in the Add to Parent Company menu, and select the Parent company from the list. The name of the Parent company appears in the Company description.

Remove a Company from a Parent Company

  1. From the Admin Panel, Company menu, click Companies.
  2. Click the (child) company name.
  3. Click Edit Details.
  4. In the Parent company drop-down menu, select a different parent, or None.
  5. Click Save.

Managing Parent / Child Company Relationships in Bulk
If you need to change the parent/child relationship for several companies, use the Actions menu. 1. Select Admin Panel > Companies 2. Click the checkbox for each company. 3. From the Actions drop-down menu, select the Parent Company (or None, to remove the Parent Company).
