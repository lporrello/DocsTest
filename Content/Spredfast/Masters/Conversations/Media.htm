Instagram Engagement by Type

The large numeral is the sum of all engagement (likes and comments) received by all auto-imported media published during the date range by all Instagram accounts in the Initiatives. Click on it to see engagement sums per account. For each engagement type (likes, comments), the top-level numbers represent the sum for all accounts; click on the number for each type to see engagement values of that type for each account.

Comments—Number of comments received by all auto-imported media, for all Instagram accounts that are part of the Initiatives. This data is provided by Instagram.

Likes—Number of likes received by all auto-imported media, for all Instagram accounts that are part of the Initiatives. This data is provided by Instagram.

Instagram Published Media

The sum of media (photos, videos, and comments) published on Instagram and then auto-imported, by all Instagram accounts in the Initiatives, over the date range. The number is the total over the date range; click on the number to see the message count per account. The graph shows the trend in publishing activity over multiple weeks; click on a week’s data point to see media published per account for a specific week.Note that comments made from the Inbox, though counted as publishing activity, do not collect engagement. This data is calculated by .

Instagram Average Engagement

The number is the average engagement value: the sum of all engagement (likes and comments) received by all media (photos and videos) auto-imported during the date range by all Instagram accounts in the Initiatives, divided by the number of items published. Click on the number to see average engagement per item published by each account. The graph shows the trend in engagement activity over multiple weeks; click on a week’s data point to see average engagement received by each account during that week. This data is calculated by .

Instagram Media by Type

Media (photos, videos) published on Instagram and then auto-imported, by all Instagram accounts in the Initiatives, over the date range, broken out by media type. Click on the section for each type to see the breakdown per account.

Instagram Label Aggregation

The labels applied to the best-performing media, based on average engagement. Includes the number of media items that used the labels and the amount of engagement (average and count) received by each. A blank in the Label field indicates that no label was used. A media item may appear in multiple counts if it has multiple labels applied to it. Click on the label name, average engagement, engagement count, or count of published media value to see a table of the media items that comprise the data. These values are calculated by .

Instagram Labels by Average Engagement

Bar graph shows proportional average engagement received by media containing each label; rollover the colored bar to see label name and the average amount of engagement received by media that included it; click to see the average engagement received by each media item. This data is calculated by .

Instagram Labels by Published Media

Bar graph shows proportional usage of each label; rollover the colored bar to see label name and the number of media items that included it; click to see the list of media containing the label, and the engagement they received. Note that a blank in the Label Name field means no label was used. This data is calculated by .

Instagram Media by Engagement

The top 50 individual media items (photos or videos) based on the amount of engagement (count) they received, across all accounts in the Initiatives. (Note that when there are duplicates of a media item in the list, it often means the item was published through multiple Initiatives.) Click on the text in the Text field to see the Initiatives the message was published in; then, in the Media column, click the ID entry to go to the Message Details page for the item. These values are calculated by .

If you want to see more than 50 messages, use the Media Explorer.

Insights: Instagram Post Type

Provides the most successful media (photo or video) for the channel during the date range, based on the average engagement (engagement count divided by number of media items posted). This data is calculated by .

Insights: Instagram Post Day

The best day of the week to post media on the channel based on the average engagement (engagement count divided by number of media posted) for the date range. This data is calculated by .

Insights: Instagram Post Hour

The best time of the day (in Pacific time) to post media on the channel, based on the average engagement (engagement count divided by number of messages posted) for the date range. This data is calculated by .
