Schedule Reports

  • Schedule Reports enables you to see all of the reports in your system and to whom they are being sent. You can also delete any of the scheduled reports in your system.
  • To delete a report, click the trash can icon (Delete) or the report that you want to delete, and then, in the confirmation dialog that appears, click the Delete button.
  • If you want to modify a report, delete it and then recreate it, from the Analytics page, with the new parameters that you want it to feature.
  • If you receive an error when trying to delete a report, ensure that you are logged in, and then try again.
