Highlighted Stream

The Highlighted stream contains items that have been highlighted for you and that you have highlighted (for yourself or others). Items that are highlighted for you (by yourself or others) are shown with a pale yellow background. Items that are highlighted for others are shown with a white background.

If you are a member of a team, and an item has been highlighted for the team, it appears in your Highlighted stream with a pale yellow background. Actions taken on highlighted items are summarized in the Details panel and shown in the hover text over the action type. (For example, recent Highlight activity is summarized when you hover over the Highlights entry in the Details panel.)

You can only highlight items for users whose Workspaces include access to the Inbox.

Use the Show Items menu in the Stream Management menu (chevron icon) at the top of the Highlighted stream to change the type of items displayed in the stream.

You can select from the following:

  • All highlighted items
  • All highlighted for me
  • All highlighted by me
  • All unaccepted items (highlighted for me individually, plus those highlighted for any highlight teams I am on)
  • All my unaccepted items (highlighted individually for me)
  • All unaccepted items for my Highlight team(s)
  • My accepted items

Show Items works together with the regular stream filter (Stream Management (chevron icon) > Add filter); for example, you can apply a date range filter, then apply an All Unaccepted Items quick filter to the results.

From the Highlighted steam, you can accept and release items; the accepted status appears in the Details panel for the stream item. You can only accept items that have been highlighted for you (as an individual or as a member of a highlight team), and that have not been accepted by another user. You cannot accept items that appear in your Highlighted stream because you’ve highlighted them for other users.

For more information on using the Highlighted stream to manage conversations, see Highlight.

You can move items from the Highlighted stream into your personal archive (Archive), and return them to the Highlighted stream using Restore from Archive.
