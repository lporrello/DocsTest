Accounts

Followers

The sum of Twitter Followers for all Twitter accounts in the Initiatives; click on the number to see the sum for each Twitter account. The graph shows the trend in Follower count over multiple weeks; click on a week’s data point to see the Follower counts for each account for that specific week. This data is provided by Twitter.

Mentions

The number of tweets that contained the handle of an authenticated Twitter account included in the Initiatives. This count does not include Replies or Editable Retweets, as Twitter's Mentions metric does. The graph shows the trend in Mentions over multiple weeks; click on a week's data point to see the number for each account.The number is the change over the date range; click to see the change in Mentions per account. This data is calculated by .

Direct Messages

The number of Direct Messages received by all Twitter accounts authenticated in that are part of the Initiatives. The graph shows the trend in Direct Messages over multiple weeks; click on a week’s data point to see the number for each account during that week. The number is the change over the date range; click it to see the change in Direct Messages per account. This data is provided by Twitter.

Mention Followers

Tracks the follower count of any handle that @mentions your handle, not counting editable retweets. The graph shows the trend in Mention Followers over multiple weeks; click on a week’s data point to see the number for each account during that week. The number is the change over the date range; click it to see the change in Mention Followers per account. This data is calculated by Spredfast.

Twitter Accounts by Engagement

Metric

Definition

Engagement

The sum of engagement (Clicks, Replies, Favorites, Retweets (editable and “native”) received over the date range by the Twitter accounts that are part of the Initiatives This value is calculated by .

# of Messages

The sum of tweets posted by the account over the date range.

Followers

The number of Followers the account has as of the last week in the date range.

Mentions

The change in the number of Twitter Mentions the account received, over the date range.

Direct Messages

The change in the number of Direct Messages the account received, over the date range.

Mention Followers

The change in the number of Followers of people who mentioned the Twitter account, over the date range. If the user Mentions the account twice, the Follower count is included twice. This value is calculated by .
