Video Explorer

This table enables you to look at individual video posts by the date and time that they were published.

The Video Explorer tab includes Video Explorer table with the following values:

  • Date
  • Time Pac/US
  • Account
  • Labels
  • Video ID
  • Video
  • Dark
  • Text (Click to drill to Video Detail)
  • Total Views
  • Organic Views
  • Paid Views
  • Total View (to 95%)
  • Organic Views (to 95%)
  • Paid Views (to 95%)
  • Total Unique Views
  • Unique Views Organic
  • Unique Views Paid
  • Total Unique Views (to 95%)
  • Unique Views Organic (to 95%)
  • Unique Views Paid (to 95%)
  • Total Reach
  • Organic Reach
  • Paid Reach
  • Avg. Time Watched
  • Likes
  • Comments
  • Shared
