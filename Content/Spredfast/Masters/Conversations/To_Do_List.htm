To-Do List

The To-do list, organized by day, spans from two weeks in the past, to one week in the future and contains...

  • A count of items highlighted for you (does not include items highlighted for Highlight teams of which you are a member).
  • Scheduled publishing events for which you are assignee, and some action is required—the message has a status of Needs Content, Error, Draft, Rejected, Needs approval.

Items older than two weeks drop off from the list. If items are deleted from the Agenda, they disappear from the list as well. Archiving a Highlighted item removes it from the list.
