Social Care Flexible Report Configuration

The Social Care Flexible Report Configuration pane allows you track the data that matters to your team. Conversations automatically generates reports based on your Care Team users and labels. Customize your analytics by setting up additional count-based or time-based reports on your inbound labeled content.

  • Count Reports—contain the raw count of items with or without the specific label(s). For each selected label, how many items were assigned that label, per day. Available for “Inbound” labels only.
  • Duration Reports—track the time between label applications. These reports record the length of time between two selected labels or states (open to accepted, or accepted to resolved, and the like). Available for “Inbound” labels only.
  • Outbound vs. Inbound—“Outbound” refers to labels assigned to outgoing activity, such as labels put on a Facebook microblog or Twitter tweet. “Inbound” refers to labels assigned to items from within the Inbox.
