Volume

The Volume tab shows how your group applied labels across different networks and at what frequency. The # Labeled Items pane shows the number of inbound items that received a label(s).  The # Labeled Items Last Period shows the number of inbound items that received a label(s) over previous period of time. The Change Over Range (up or down) is calculated by comparing the data from the current time period to the data from the prior (equivalent) time period.

Volume by Channel breaks down each channel to a raw count. Volume by Day, Volume by Day of Week, and Volume by Hour shows the breakdown across channel per increment of time. Click any part of the graph to see itemized lists of content. The Labeled Items Heatmap shows the number of items that received labels based on their original time and date.

Top Labels shows the most frequently used labels based on date. Click the value for any point to see an itemized list of responses. Users by Labels shows the distribution of labels per user. Click the section for any period to see an itemized list of responses.
