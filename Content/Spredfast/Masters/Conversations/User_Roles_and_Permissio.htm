User Roles and Permissions

A user’s ability to see and use features in depends on the following:

  • her role, which determines what actions she can take in the system
  • the Initiatives she is in, which determine the projects she has access to
  • the Account Sets she has access to, which determine the social accounts she can use to publish
  • the workspace she has, which determines the screens and features that appear to her when she uses the web application

To access the Roles and Permissions page, go to [User Name], and then select My Company > Users > Roles.

There are five standard roles in ; these can be identified by the icon to the left of the name. These roles cannot be deleted. Each has a set of default abilities; these appear in the What Users Can Do pane when the role is selected from the drop-down menu. Two of the functional areas (Content Center and Promote) can be edited, resulting in a custom role. To create a custom role that involves change in the other functional areas (Publishing, for example), you must contact Support.

Admins can create custom roles using all of the functional areas.

Once created, custom roles can be assigned to users. They can be edited and resaved.

Create a Custom Role

  1. From the My Company > Users tab, click Roles.
  2. From the drop-down menu, select a standard role to use as a baseline.
  3. Make changes to the Content Center permissions or Promotion permissions.
  4. Admins can make changes to all functional areas.
  5. Click Save As, and provide a name for the custom role.
  6. Click Save As.

On the Roles and Permissions page, click Save.

Delete a Custom Role

  1. From the My Company > Users tab, click Roles.
  2. From the drop-down menu, click the custom role you want to delete.
  3. Click Delete Role.
  4. In the confirmation dialog, click Delete.
