Content Promotion

Select Facebook Pages for paid content promotion and associate Facebook Pages with Facebook Ad Accounts.

Note that only Company Admins or users who have been given Promotion privileges are allowed to promote content on Facebook Pages from .

See User Roles and Permissions.

Facebook Pages must already be authenticated in and must be set to Published status in Facebook to be eligible for content promotion. To enable Facebook pages, a user must be an administrator on the Facebook Page and have access to at least one Ad Account in Facebook. Each Facebook Page must be associated with a single Facebook ad account within , but an ad account can be used for multiple Pages.

Configure Facebook Content Promotion

  1. Select [Your name] > My Company > Content Promotion.
  2. Click Add page.
  3. Log in using a Facebook account that is both a Page Administrator on the Facebook Pages, and has access to a Facebook Ad Account.
  4. When asked for Permissions, click Allow.
  5. In the Enable Facebook Pages for Promotion (1) dialog, click the checkbox to choose pages that will support Content Promotion from .

Criteria for Accounts to Appear in the List
Based on the login credentials, displays a list of accounts that meet the following criteria:
1. Have Ad Accounts already associated with them in Facebook.
2) Have already been authenticated in .
If a page does not meet both conditions, it is not available in the list.

Reassign Ad Accounts and Facebook Users to Facebook Pages

  1. If multiple Facebook accounts have been authenticated in for content promotion, you can change which Ad Account and User accounts are associated with a Page.
  2. Select [Your name] > My Company > Content Promotion.
  3. Select Facebook Pages
  4. Find the page you want to reassign, and then click the keys icon (Edit).
  5. In the Facebook Page Ad Settings dialog, use the Ad Account selector to change the account, and the User account selector to change the Facebook user associated with the Page.
  6. Click Save Changes.

Disable Content Promotion for a Facebook Page

Disabling a page removes it from the Content Promotion list, so that no Facebook ads can be promoted on it from . Note that if there are promotions that are active or pending on the Facebook page, it cannot be disabled.

  1. Select [Your name] > My Company > Content Promotion.
  2. Select Facebook Pages.
  3. Find the page you want to disable, and click the trash can icon (Delete ).
  4. In the Disable Content Promotion dialog, click Disable.
