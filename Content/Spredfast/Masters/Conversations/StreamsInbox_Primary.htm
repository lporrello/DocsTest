Primary Collection

The Primary Collection is a special collection of streams, visible to all users with access to the Initiative. It contains the most important streams—the ones that everyone working in the Initiative needs to read. An orange dot is used to mark unread items.

As of April 2014 (the 5.4 release) is making some internal data processing changes that may influence the items that appear in your Primary Collection stream. Historically, items appeared in the Primary Collection as of the date a stream was added to the Inbox (no items from before that date were included). After the change, historical items (published before the date the stream was added to the Inbox, up to the last 100 days) will also appear. Users will see these changes take effect in the weeks following the release; the items will appear in the stream as “Unread.”

Change the Streams that Appear in the Primary Collection

  1. In the Stream Library, find the Primary Collection, and click the pen icon (Edit). The Edit Primary Collection pane appears.
  2. Find the stream in the Stream Library. Drag the stream into the target area at the bottom of the Edit Primary Collection dialog. Repeat for each stream you want to include.
  3. Click Save.

Or

  1. At the top of the Primary Collection stream, click the Stream Management (chevron) icon, and choose Edit Primary Collection.
  2. In the Primary Collection Settings window, pick one or more streams to add.
  3. Click Save.
