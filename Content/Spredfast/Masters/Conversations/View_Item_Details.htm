View Item Details

To show the Item Details panels, you can...

  • Click in any open area of a stream item
  • Click on the More Actions menu and select Item Details,
  • Click View All from within a Comment entry
  • Click View Conversation from within a response entry.

Item Details: Item

On the Item Details: Item page, you can see an audit trail (Log) of actions that users have taken on the item, and notes users have made. Each item that has already been responded to, or for which a response has been scheduled, is given a log entry.

If the item is from a Brandwatch, Sysomos, or Synthesio stream, an additional pane appears below the Labels section. This pane contains the Brandwatch, Sysomos, or Synthesio data.

The full set of item responses are available from the Item tab.

Item Details: Conversation

If a stream item is part of a conversation on a channel (part of a series of messages and replies), a bar appears at the top of the item that links to the Conversation tab.

For channels other than Twitter, the Conversation tab shows the original message at the top of the stream, and the associated responses in chronological order.

For Twitter, The Conversation tab contains all previous messages in the conversation, plus any messages that were published through .

For Twitter, Previous Items Only
The Conversation tab for Twitter shows items published before the current item (the one you used to access the Conversation tab) plus any items published through ; it cannot contain items (responses) published after the current item. To be sure that you see the entire conversation, you must be in the most recent item posted in the conversation.

The Conversation tab can contain up to 10 items; if the thread is longer, an option to Load Earlier Conversation appears. You can change the order of the items by clicking Newest on Top or Oldest on Top.

Within the tab, the original message appears with a white background, and the current item (the one used to access the Conversation tab) appears with a dark gray background.

From the bottom of each item, a channel-appropriate set of Response and Processing options are provided. For items published through , these options won’t appear until the message has published and brought into the app.
