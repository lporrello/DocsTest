Custom Channels

Create “custom” channels to track social activity that is not published through on the Calendar. Once a custom channel and content type has been configured, you can create non-publishing “message” placeholders that appear in the Calendar along with your scheduled content. See Week View.

Create a Custom Channel

  1. Select [Your name] > My Company > Custom Channel.
  2. Click Add Channel.
  3. In the Display Name field, provide a name.
  4. In the Icon field, upload an icon to represent the channel on the Calendar.
  5. In the Content Type field, either
  6. Select a type from the drop-down menu, or
  7. Click Custom Content Type, and, in the Custom Type field, provide a name.
  8. In the Contact Name field, provide a name, email address, or other appropriate contact information.
  9. Click Create.
