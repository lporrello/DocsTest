Accounts

Followers

The total number of Followers for all LinkedIn Company Pages in the Initiatives. The number is the most recent count; click on the number to see the number of Followers per page. The graph shows the trend in Follower count over multiple weeks; click on a week’s data point to see the count for each page during a specific week. This data is provided by LinkedIn.

Connections

The total number of connections for all LinkedIn Profiles in the Initiatives. The number is the most recent count; click on the number to see counts for each Profile. The graph shows the trend in connection over multiple weeks; click on a week’s data point to see the count for each Profile during a specific week. This data is provided by LinkedIn.

LinkedIn-Followers by Industry

The top industries your Followers work in; the Industries count is the number of individual Industries represented by your Followers. Click on the value (numeral) to see the number of Followers in each industry category. Not all Followers provide industry data to LinkedIn.

LinkedIn-Followers by Country

The countries your Followers are from. The Countries count is the number of individual countries represented by your Followers; click on the total to see the Follower count by country. Not all Followers provide country data to LinkedIn.

LinkedIn-Followers by Company Size

The size of companies your Followers work for; the Company Sizes count is number of size categories represented by your Followers. Click on the total to see the breakdown and number of Followers for each category. Note that the categories and subdivisions are provided and defined by LinkedIn. A category only appears on this page if there is data for it; empty categories/subdivisions are not displayed in the chart. Not all Followers provide company size data to LinkedIn.

LinkedIn-Followers by Function

The Function your Followers perform in their organization. The Functions count is the number of Function categories represented by your Followers. Click on the number to see the number of people in each category. Not all Followers provide function data to LinkedIn.

LinkedIn-Followers by Seniority

The level of professional seniority held by your Followers. The Seniorities count is number of seniority categories represented by your Followers. Click on the number to see the number of people in each category. Not all Followers provide seniority data to LinkedIn.

LinkedIn-Followers by Employees

The Follower count, divided into employee and non-employees.

LinkedIn-Accounts by Engagement

The best-performing LinkedIn accounts, based on the amount of engagement (count) received by their messages. Click on the # of Messages entry to see detailed engagement data per message.
