Comment

Please note, company accounts cannot comment on Company updates.

You can add a simple comment by clicking the Comment icon, typing in the content field, and clicking Comment. will use the account that originally created the stream to publish it. Or, click the More options button to use an expanded set of publishing features (other accounts, scheduling, notes, labels, etc.) for your post.

Like a LinkedIn Post

From the stream item, click Like and choose accounts. Once an item has been liked via , it can be unliked as well. The history of “Like” actions taken in is shown in the stream item entry (account, date, and time). Note that the Likes count is not the total number of times the item was liked on LinkedIn; it is the number of times the item was liked from .

Reply (As an Individual or a Company).

You can add a simple reply by clicking the Reply icon, typing in the content field, and clicking Reply. Or, click the More options button to use an expanded set of publishing features (other accounts, scheduling, notes, labels, etc.) for your post. By default, the Company is used to reply, but you can select individual user accounts instead.

Star (Group Stream Only)

Delete comments from LinkedIn Groups discussion topics or LinkedIn Company pages.
