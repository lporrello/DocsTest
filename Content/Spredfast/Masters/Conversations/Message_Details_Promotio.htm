Message Details—Promotion

For Facebook Promoted messages, the Promotion tab provides details about the promotion, such as status, Paid Reach, Budget Spent, Time Elapsed, and Duration. Within the Promotion tab, metrics are updated from Facebook once per hour.

The top section of the tab provides a summary of all ads while the subsequent sections provide details for each ad.

Promotions Tab Promotion Summary Fields
Field Definition
Pending The total number of pending promotions.
Complete The total number of promotions completed.
Active The total number of active promotions.
Failed The number of failed promotions.
Paid Reach The total number of individual viewers who saw the ad.
Budget Spent The total amount spent.
Budget The total budget allocated.
Time Elapsed The amount of time that has passed since the ad went live.

 

Promotions Tab Promotion Details Fields
Field Definition
Paid Reach How many individual viewers saw the promotion.
Budget Spent The amount spent to date on the promotion.
Time Elapsed How long the promotion has been running.
Est. Reach An estimate of how many views you'll reach with this targeting promotion and spend.
Budget Allotted The allotted budget.
Starting When the promotion started or is set to start.
Ending When the promotion is set to end.
Duration The total length of the promotion.
[Status] Indicates the status of the promotion.
