Pinterest Average Engagement

The number is the average engagement value: the sum of all engagement (Repins, Likes, and comments) received by all pins, published through and auto-imported for all Pinterest Boards in the Initiatives divided by the number of pins published. Click on the number to see average engagement per item published by each Board. The graph shows the trend in engagement activity over multiple weeks; click on a week’s data point to see average engagement received by each Board during that week.This data is calculated by .

Pinterest Engagement by Type

The large numeral is the sum of all engagement (Repins, Likes, and comments) received by all pins, published through and auto-imported for all Pinterest Boards in the Initiatives, over the date range. Click on it to see engagement sums per Board. For each engagement type (Repins, Likes, comments), the top-level numbers represent the sum for all Boards; click on the number for each type to see engagement values of that type for each Board.

  • Repins—Number of Repins received by all pins published through and auto-imported, for all Pinterest Boards that are part of the Initiatives. This data is provided by Pinterest.
  • Likes—Number of Likes received by all pins published through and auto-imported, for all Pinterest Boards that are part of the Initiatives. This data is provided by Pinterest.
  • Comments—Number of comments received by all pins published through and auto-imported, for all Pinterest Boards that are part of the Initiatives. This data is provided by Pinterest.

Pinterest Label Aggregation

The labels applied to the best-performing pins, based on average engagement. Includes the number of published pins that used the labels and the amount of engagement (average and count) received by each. A blank in the Label field indicates that no label was used. A pin may appear in multiple counts if it has multiple labels applied to it. Click on the label name, average engagement, engagement count, or count of published pins value to see a table of the pins that comprise the data. These values are calculated by .

Pinterest Pins by Engagement

The top 50 individual pins based on the amount of engagement (count) they received, across all Boards in the Initiatives. (Note that when there are duplicates of a pin in the list, it often means the pin was published through multiple Initiatives.) Click on the text in the Text field to see the Initiatives the message was published in; then, in the Pin column, click the ID entry to go to the Message Details page for the item. These values are calculated by .

If you want to see more than 50 messages, use the Pin Explorer.
