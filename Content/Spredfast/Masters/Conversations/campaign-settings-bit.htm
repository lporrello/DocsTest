Link Shortening

supports link shortening by Bitly and BudURL. Both are configured similarly.

Add a custom Bitly or BudURL account for link shortening:

  1. From Initiative Settings, click the Link Shortening tab.
  2. Click 's Bitly Account or Authenticate Your Own Account.
  3. Use the pull-down control to select Bitly or BudURL.
  4. Click Authenticate and Save to authenticate your account, and then sign in using your account.
  5. Click Allow.

Bitly

By default, uses ’s Bitly account to shorten the links embedded in social media content. If your organization prefers to use its own Bitly account, authenticate it here and will use it both to shorten your links and to report click-through metrics on auto-imported content. Each Initiative can have one account associated with it.

Bitly uses OAuth to authenticate accounts. If Bitly cannot be accessed during publishing, publishes the long URL instead. In this case, click metrics cannot be collected on the message.

BudURL

When you configure BudURL for your initiative, will no longer use the default Bitly account to shorten links but will instead use their BudURL account (and short link domain. BudURL provides the following support:

  • Shortening links via BudURL at publish time.
  • Counting clicks on links shortened by BudURL in content (published by or auto-imported).
  • You can see additional metrics at app.budurl.com.

What API URL Should I Use?
The API URL must match the field called "API Primary Base URL", or the integration will not work. Do not use sfast.budurl.com.
