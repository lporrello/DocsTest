Google+ Messages (Count)

The sum of messages published by all Google+ accounts in the Initiatives, over the date range. The number is the total over the date range; click on the number to see the message count per account. The graph shows the trend in publishing activity over multiple weeks; click on a week’s data point to see messages published per account for a specific week. This data is calculated by .

Google+ Average Engagement

The number is the average engagement value: the sum of all engagement (clicks, comments, +1s, reshares) received by all messages published during the date range by all Google+ accounts in the Initiatives, divided by the number of messages published. Click on the number to see average engagement for messages published by each account. The graph shows the trend in engagement activity over multiple weeks; click on a week’s data point to see average engagement received by each account during that week. This data is calculated by .

Google+ Engagement by Type

The large numeral is the sum of all engagement (clicks, comments, +1s, reshares) received by all messages published during the date range by all Google+ accounts in the Initiatives. Click on it to see engagement sums per account. For each engagement type (Clicks, Comments, +1s, Reshares), the top-level numbers represent the sum for all accounts; click on the number for each type to see engagement values of that type for each account.

Clicks—Number of clicks made on (Bitly) links embedded in messages published via or auto-imported, for all Google+ accounts that are part of the Initiatives. This data is provided by Bitly.

Comments—Number of comments received by all messages published via or auto-imported, for all Google+ accounts that are part of the Initiatives. This data is provided by Google.

+1s—Number of +1s received by all messages published via or auto-imported, for all Google+ accounts that are part of the Initiatives. This data is provided by Google.

Reshares—Number of reshares received by messages published via or auto-imported, for all Google+ accounts that are part of the Initiatives. This data is provided by Google.

Google+ Engagement Without Clicks

Proportional display of the sums of Comments, +1s and Reshares received by all messages published during the date range by all Google+ accounts in the Initiatives.

Google+ Label Aggregation

The labels applied to the best-performing messages, based on average engagement. Includes the number of messages that used the labels and the amount of engagement (average and count) received by the messages. A blank in the Label field indicates that no label was used. A message may appear in multiple counts if it has multiple labels applied to it. Click on the label name, average engagement, engagement count, or # of Messages value to see a table of the messages that comprise the data. These values are calculated by .
