Email, Password, and Picture

When you first log in to , the name, your email address and company fields are pre-populated using the information provided by the administrator who added you to the system.

A user’s ability to modify his password and email address in depends on the Security Settings established by the company. (See Password and Account Security.) If you can change your password, you must enter your current password to do so. The new password will be required the next time you log in to .

To add a picture to your user profile, click Update Image and upload an image file (JPEG, GIF, or PNG file formats). To change the image, click Update Image and upload a replacement file. You cannot return to a “no image” profile once you have added a picture; you can only change the image file.
