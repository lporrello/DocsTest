Available Account

For an account to appear in this list, it must exist on the social channel (Facebook, LinkedIn, etc.), have been authenticated within , and you must have access to the Account Set associated with the account.

To authenticate a social media account in means to provide credentials (such as user name/password) so that you can use to take actions that you would ordinarily take on the channel itself (such as posting, commenting, responding, and the like).

In , a Account Set is a collection of social media accounts. When you are set up as a user in , your administrator gives you access to appropriate Account Sets.

To learn more about authentication and Account Sets in , refer to the User Guide.
