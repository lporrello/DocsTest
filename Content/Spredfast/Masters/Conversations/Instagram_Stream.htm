Instagram Streams

  1. From the Stream Library menu, select Create Streams.
  2. Select Instagram from the drop-down menu.
  3. In the Select an available account list, select an authenticated Instagram account.
  4. For My Photos and Videos, or Timeline Activity streams: In the Add a stream to your Inbox panel, select the stream type, and click Add.
  5. For Hashtag streams, click Hashtags, then, in the # field, enter the text of the hashtag, and click Add.
  6. Click Done.
