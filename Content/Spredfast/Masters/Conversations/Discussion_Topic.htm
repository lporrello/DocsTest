Discussion Topic

LinkedIn Group Discussion Fields
Field Description
Title Name of the Discussion topic
Insert Link Provide an embedded (trackable) link (to bring users to your site or other designated location)
Summary Provide content for the discussion
Account Select LinkedIn Group accounts on which to post the topic; no accounts are pre-selected.
Announce Click the checkbox to announce the topic on microblog channels.
Template Add the topic title and URL to the announcement
Groups/Channels Select the channels
Labels Choose labels to apply to the discussion topic within .
