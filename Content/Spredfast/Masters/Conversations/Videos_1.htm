Videos

This tab provides aggregate analytics, for all video post, covering the following elements for videos in the date range that you defined.

The Videos tab includes the following:

  • Number of published Videos
  • Total number of Views
  • Views Over 30 Seconds or 95%
  • Likes
  • Comments
  • Shares
  • Post Clicks
  • Negative Feedback
  • Number of Views at Each Interval (table)
  • Organic and Paid Views with Engagement (table)
  • Top Videos by Engagement (table)
  • Top Videos by Views (table)
