Show Items from Other Initiatives on Your Calendar

By default, the Calendar shows items from your current, active Initiative. Use the menu options available on the Basic and Advanced filters to add other Initiatives. To create a company-wide calendar, make sure you have access to all Initiatives in the company.

To add other Initiatives to your Calendar:

  1. From the Calendar screen, select Filters > Basic (or Advanced) filter.
  2. In the Select Other Initiatives drop-down menu (upper right-hand corner of the dialog), use the checkboxes to select Initiatives to include on the Calendar (your current, active Initiative is already included and doesn’t appear in the list). You can also select All, or use the None option to clear your selections.
  3. Click Apply. The Calendar refreshes; Items from another Initiative show the Initiative name in italics.
