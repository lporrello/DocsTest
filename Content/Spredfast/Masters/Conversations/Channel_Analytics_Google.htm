Channel Analytics—Google+

This section covers channel analytics available for Google+.

Due to the way that Google+ provides data through their API, there are the following limits to metrics data and auto-import in :

  • Metrics are collected on only the 500 most recent Google+ posts to a page (approximately).
  • Only the 200 most recent posts to a Google+ page are auto-imported (approximately).

These numbers are approximate because Google+ provides posts out of order. We might collect metrics for, or auto-import, older items instead of newer items in some cases. We will collect metrics for 500 and auto-import 200, but they may not always be precisely the most recent.
